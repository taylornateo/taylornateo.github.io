Target Audience

My target audience for my Ginger forecast site is all people with fair skin. No person will fair skin enjoys goin outside if there is a chance to get a bad sunburn. This site will all everyone to know whether or not they need sun block. It should tell you what the weather will be like on certain days so that they can determine when to head out and what day would be better to stay in.

Personas

Freddy

Freddy is an avid outdoorsman that is always on the move. Freddy is also a "ginger". His friends can him the "Day Walker." He love camping and hiking but hates sunburns. In order to prepare for a long day in the sun he will always check the weather to see how it will be outside while he out and about. So it is crucial for him to know how it will be outside.

Jill

Jill is a scenic photographer and is always looking for new places to find to take pictures. Each day she sets out to new spots in the town or out in hiking trails to find interesting scenery that can be used for a good photograph. She is constantly checking the weather to see how the weather will affect her picture taking that day. She likes to know if it will be cloudy or sunny outside.

Scenarios

  • A tourist is taking pictures in the downtown, when it starts raining. He needs to take shelter and check when the rain will stop.
  • Freddy is want to go to an outdoor event. He needs to see when the prime time to go hike is.
  • Jill is looking for a new way to take pictures. She is out taking pictures and can use the weather to her advantage.
  • Freddy wants to go to the beach with friends. He want to know what type of protection he needs to bring to stay there as long as he can.
  • Jill wants to go swimming today but it looks like it might rain today, will it?
