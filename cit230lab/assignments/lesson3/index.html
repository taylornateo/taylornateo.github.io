Purpose

The purpose of the "Viewfinder Forecast" site is to bring up to date weather information in select areas of the Idaho area. My purpose for this weather site in specific will be to create a site for photographers looking for weather information in the area. I will create for this target audience so they can know when the best times are to go out and take pictures in the areas they live in.
