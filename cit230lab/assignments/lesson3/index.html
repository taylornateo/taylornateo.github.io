Purpose

The purpose of the "The Ginger Forecast" site is to bring up to date weather information in select areas of the Idaho area. My purpose for this weather site will be to create a site for people with fair skin types looking for weather information in the area. I will create for this target audience so they can know it is safe for them to come outside to play, or if it would be better to stay out of the sun.
